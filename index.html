MISSION

To foster the personal and professional development of ambitious Black and Latino college students through direct and intense coaching

PROGRAM

REQUIREMENTS

  • Identify as Black/African-American or Hispanic/Latino
  • Attending an accredited college in the New York City metro area (NJ/CT included)
  • Majoring in Accounting, Economics, Finance, or another financial services-related topic
  • Cumulative GPA of 3.3 (sophomore), 3.5 (junior), 3.7 (senior); freshmen do not have a required minimum GPA
